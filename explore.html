AURORA EAST

East Sikkim

Experience the mesmerizing allure of East Sikkim, where the mystical charm of the Himalayas meets the vibrant culture of the region. Explore ancient monasteries nestled amidst rolling hills, stroll through quaint villages adorned with colorful prayer flags, and witness the breathtaking vistas of snow-capped peaks. From the bustling streets of Gangtok to the tranquil retreats of Tsomgo Lake, East Sikkim offers a perfect blend of adventure and serenity.

MYSTIC WEST

West Sikkim

Explore the mystical allure of West Sikkim, where lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and serene monasteries await your discovery. Embark on scenic hikes to ancient temples, immerse yourself in the vibrant local culture, and rejuvenate your spirit amidst the breathtaking landscapes of this hidden gem.

VERDANT SOUTH

South Sikkim

Immerse yourself in the lush landscapes and tranquil beauty of South Sikkim. From the emerald green valleys to the mist-covered hills, this region offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Explore ancient monasteries, embark on picturesque treks, and experience the rich biodiversity of this verdant paradise.

ZENITH NORTH

North Sikkim

Embark on an unforgettable journey to North Sikkim, where snow-capped peaks, pristine lakes, and breathtaking landscapes await. Traverse rugged terrain, witness cascading waterfalls, and encounter the vibrant culture of the indigenous tribes. Whether you're seeking adventure or serenity, North Sikkim offers a truly immersive experience amidst the grandeur of the Himalayas.
